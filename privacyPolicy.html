Privacy Policy for Combo Creator

Last updated: November 7, 2025

Developer: Tony Jordan

Contact email: combocreator.dev@gmail.com

1. Introduction

Combo Creator (“the App”) is developed and maintained by Tony Jordan (“the Developer”). This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the App. Combo Creator respects your privacy and does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Combo Creator is designed to run entirely offline and does not collect or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information.

We do not collect location data.

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies.

We do not access your device’s camera, microphone, or files.

3. Use of the App

All data generated within the App (such as strike sets, combinations, or favorites) remains stored locally on your device. You have full control over this data and may delete it at any time by uninstalling the App.

4. Children’s Privacy

Combo Creator is suitable for users of all ages. However, children should use the App under parental supervision. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children or anyone else.

5. Third-Party Services

Combo Creator does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or cloud storage providers.

6. Security

Because Combo Creator does not collect or transmit data over the internet, there is minimal risk of data exposure. All user-generated content remains securely stored on your own device.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after any modification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
